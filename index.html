    About the Attachment Style Test

    How the Chat Simulator Works

    This test uses realistic text message conversations instead of traditional questions. You chat through 10 scenarios with a virtual partner, and your natural response choices reveal your attachment style. The chat format captures authentic relationship patterns that standard questionnaires often miss.

    Understanding Attachment Theory

    Attachment theory, developed by John Bowlby and Mary Ainsworth, explains how early bonding experiences shape adult relationship patterns. The four styles -- Secure, Anxious, Avoidant, and Fearful-Avoidant -- influence how you communicate, handle conflict, seek closeness, and respond to your partner's needs.
